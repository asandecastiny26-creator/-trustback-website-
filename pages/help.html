Frequently Asked Questions

Most refunds are issued within 21 days if you filed electronically and chose direct deposit. Paper returns can take 6-8 weeks to process. You can check your refund status using our "Where's My Refund?" tool.

You can request tax transcripts online through your TrustBack account, by phone at 1-800-908-9946, or by mail using Form 4506-T. Online requests are processed immediately, while phone and mail requests take 5-10 business days.

If you can't pay your full tax bill, you should still file your return on time to avoid additional penalties. Consider setting up a payment plan, making a partial payment, or exploring other payment options like an offer in compromise.

You can report tax fraud using Form 3949-A or call 1-800-829-0433. For identity theft related to taxes, use Form 14039. All reports are confidential and help protect the integrity of the tax system.

The Taxpayer Advocate Service (TAS) is an independent organization within TrustBack that helps resolve tax problems when normal TrustBack channels haven't worked. Contact them at 1-877-777-4778 or through Form 911.
